What Do the Tiers Mean?

TIER A

Cards with a combined Avg. Cubes and Popularity within one standard deviation of the best card.

TIER B

Cards with a combined Avg. Cubes and Popularity better than or equal to the average.

TIER C

Cards with a combined Avg. Cubes and Popularity worse than average and within one standard deviation of the best card in this tier.

TIER D

Cards with a combined Avg. Cubes and Popularity worse than one standard deviation from the best card in tier C.
